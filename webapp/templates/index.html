Project "EleNa"

UMass Software Engineering• Fall 2017

Our goal was to design software that calculates and optimizes a route for bikers. However we believe we went the extra mile and did much more than that. Our backend will take two source/destination strings, construct a street graph using nodes and edges, inject grades for the edges using Google Elevations API, and perform various impedance functions on them to produce various behaviors using Dijkstras search algorithm. Waypoints are sent back in JSON formatting for the frontend to guide you through the route in a friendly manner. Our frontend can provide you elevation profiles for your route that optimize cruising, uphill, or downhill slopes.
